Student Academic Success (SAS)

Student Academic Success offers a range of academic support to RMIT students, helping them to develop skills, strategies and knowledge on how to succeed academically. In here, students can find learning support services such as:

  • 1:1 consultation with the Academic Advisors or Peer Tutors, who are academically excellent and professionally trained to guide and support students

  • Course-specific and academic skill workshops
